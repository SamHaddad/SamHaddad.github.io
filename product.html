  • Description
    The Cov-Lid helmet provides a fully sealed, rigid polymeric separation for the face, head, throat and neck. It is designed to provide the highest level of protection from the surrounding environment and is meant to be worn in conjunction with an N95 mask or surgical mask for maximum protection. In the event that the wearer does not have access to a face respirator or other mask the helmet will provide a very high level of protection from all droplets and fomites that are heavier than ambient air. The helmet floats freely encapsulating the entire head and neck and is supported by the wearerâ€™s shoulders via an elastomeric shoulder pad providing improved comfort and mobility throughout the day. The neck bib provides an easy method to put the helmet on and take the helmet off without any disassembly. Air intake and exhaust occurs at the back of the neck meaning that infectious particles would have to move around the neck and upwards towards the face in order to penetrate the mouth, nose, or eyes. The helmet provides top and back of head protection removing the need for other protective hoods.
  • Additonal Features
    • Maximum protection other than PPE that is pressure sealed to the face or a full hazmat suit.

  • Testing and Approvals
    Initial lab testing has shown that the helmet is very effective at providing high levels of protection from droplets and other fomites. We are currently working with the FDA and CDC (NIOSH) to obtain federal approvals.
  • Production Capacity
    Our factory is currently retooling for mass production of the Cov-Lid Helmet. We will be able to produce and deliver 10,000 units per week in April, ramping up to 30,000 units per week by the end of May.
  • Material
    The helmet is made from chemically resistant polymers (PETG and Neoprene). The upper shield is thermoformed in PETG and the neck bib is laser cut in Neoprene sheet. The shoulder harness is also made from Neoprene.
  • Disinfecting process
    The helmet can be disinfected with chemical disinfectants or soap and hot water.
  • Sizing
    The helmet is one sizes fits all with adjustable shoulder harness for all body types and sizes.
